What is Pandex?

Pandex is a pre-launch hub for original arena concepts, creator previews and future downloads for platform-fighter communities.

Pandex helps fan-made arena concepts become easier to discover, understand and follow. The site currently shows original preview artwork while the download and creator workflows are still being prepared.

A platform for original arena concepts

Pandex starts with previews: clear presentation, creator context and deeper pages for original arena concepts. Downloads are planned for a later phase after review rules, takedown handling and creator approval workflows are stable.

Built for the platform-fighter modding community

Pandex serves players and creators who care about custom arena art, readable stage silhouettes and fan-made ideas around platform fighters such as Brawlhalla. Pandex is not affiliated with Ubisoft, Blue Mammoth Games or Brawlhalla.

How creators publish on Pandex

Creators can prepare original, rights-cleared artwork and send it for review through the Submit page. Pandex reviews submissions for originality, quality and fit before anything is published.

What's coming next

The next public milestone is Phase 2: downloads for approved original creator-made arena concepts. See the Roadmap for current target windows.
