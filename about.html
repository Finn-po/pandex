About Pandex

Pandex is a pre-launch hub for original creator-made arena concepts and future downloads.

What Pandex is

Pandex is an independent fan-content project for original arena previews made by creators. The current public version focuses on visual concepts for platform-fighter communities while the broader platform is still being shaped.

Why Pandex exists

Pandex exists to give original creator-made arena concepts a clean, understandable place to be shown before downloads are available. The goal is to present work clearly, separate previews from future playable releases, and keep expectations honest while the project grows.

Who it is for

Pandex is for players, community members and independent creators who are interested in original arena ideas, preview catalogs and future creator-focused workflows. It is also intended to make it easier for visitors to understand what is currently public and what is still planned.

Current stage

Pandex is currently in a pre-launch stage. The website shows original arena preview concepts only; downloads, broader creator tools and expanded submission features are planned for later phases.

What Pandex is not

Pandex is not affiliated with Ubisoft, Blue Mammoth Games or Brawlhalla. It does not claim ownership of third-party trademarks and does not host copied IP, ripped assets or unapproved third-party artwork.
