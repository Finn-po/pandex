Submit Your Map

Want your original map background on Pandex? Send your preview images and project details for review.

1
Original Content Only
Submit only content you created yourself. No anime characters, copied artwork, ripped game assets, third-party logos or other protected IP.
2
Send Preview Images
Include the map name, preview images, creator name, social links and a short description of the concept.
3
Manual Review
Pandex reviews submissions manually before anything goes live. Approved maps can be added as previews first and downloads later.
Contact:
Review the requirements before sending files. Open the creator submission guide.
